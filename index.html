About Me

My name is Stevon Wright. I am a self-taught software developer who is passionate about sports, music, and technology. I play basketball for fun, and I also play the guitar and the piano in my spare time. I am fascinated by computers, wireless networks, and videogames. I started learning to become a software developer in middle school, learning simple HTML and CSS at the time. In high school, through two online classes offered by the state, I learned the fundamentals of programming in Python. After making some games and graphical user interfaces in Python, I began to branch out, learning other languages and frameworks through online media such as YouTube videos, online forums, and blogs.
